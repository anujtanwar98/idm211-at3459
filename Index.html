About Me

My name is Anuj Tanwar. I did my Associate of Arts Graphic Design from Foothill College, California and currently I’m pursuing my Bachelor of Science User Experience & Interaction Design from Drexel University, Pennsylvania. I’ve had the opportunity to work with my college Peers. I’m very self-motivated, problem solver and enjoy working in a team or alone.
I've been interested in how people's instinctive wills and the accessibility of information can affect their choices and experience.I love thinking and designing with challenges beyond the existing rules.
